Chaincode Labs — Who are they?

  • Bitcoin research and development center

  • Employs contributors and researchers

  • Runs the Chaincode Residency and Seminars

Chaincode Seminar — What is it?

  • 5-week cohorts of 10-25 participants

  • Weekly meetings, 1-on-1 with a peer
    & large group discussion

  • Organizes cohorts and arranges guest speakers

  • Does not moderate nor lead discussions

Be your own Chaincode — What can you do?

  • Organize a group on Slack, Signal, Telegram, etc

  • Pair participants each week

  • Arrange guest speakers

  • Contribute back to the content

Get Involved — How to get started?

  • Sign up for the Chaincode Seminar

  • Organize your own!

  • Offer to help with an existing seminar

  • Contribute material, translations, etc
